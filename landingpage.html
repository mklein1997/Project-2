    • Project 2: MLB Data Analysis

    Baseball is a time honored tradition that has brought together people worldwide for over 100 years. Families have gathered around the diamond to watch their favorite kids grow into their favorite players, and those favorite players have gone on to succeed in one of America's most dominant sports arenas, the MLB. Major League baseball is comprised of 30 teams that are split up into 5 divisions. Every year those teams post stats that are tracked, and argued over, by anyone who follows the game.

    As you navigate through you will find the locations of each franchise highlighted by many comparisons that reflect the history of each team. These visualizations will show the effects a team's total salary has on the success of a franchise, as well as, features some of the statistical outputs that each team achieved annually.
